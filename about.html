About PaintPro

PaintPro is a community-focused platform that helps families calculate paint requirements accurately, reduce unnecessary paint wastage, and connect with trusted local painters and nearby paint shops.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help every household plan painting projects efficiently while supporting local painters and paint shop owners. By calculating the exact paint quantity, families can save money and reduce environmental waste.
